About the Olympiad

The WOO Olympiad is a flagship international competition designed to ignite passion for STEM subjects among school students. We believe in providing a platform where future leaders can test their intellectual limits and develop advanced problem-solving skills.

Scholastic Depth
Skill Development
Global Community

Open to students in two categories: **Epsilon (Classes 5-7)** and **Zeta (Classes 8-10)**.

Olympiads and Disciplines

Science Cluster

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and a combined Junior Science paper. Focus on application and conceptual clarity.
