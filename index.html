About WOO

The World Olympiad Organisation (WOO) conducts 7 exciting Olympiads designed for students in classes 5-10. These Olympiads are divided into two categories:

  • Epsilon: For classes 5-7
  • Zeta: For classes 8-10

Participate in our Olympiads to test your knowledge and skills!

Our Olympiads

WPO - World Physics Olympiad

Focused on physics concepts and problem-solving.

WCO - World Chemistry Olympiad

Exploring chemical reactions, elements, and more.

WBO - World Biology Olympiad

Diving into life sciences and biological systems.

WSO - World Science Olympiad

Comprehensive science (PCB) with lower difficulty questions.

WMO - World Maths Olympiad

Challenging mathematical problems and proofs.

WJSO - World Junior Science Olympiad

Integrating science, maths, and experiments.

WHO - World Hackathon Olympiad

Coding and problem-solving in a hackathon format.

Epsilon Category (Classes 5-7)

  • 25 SCQ questions (+2/-0.5)
  • 5 MCQ questions (+4/-2)
  • 45 minutes test
  • 25 SCQ (+2/-0.5)
  • 5 MCQ (+4/-2)
  • 1 Proof (0 ≤ x ≤ 10) with 2 alternatives
  • 1 hour test
  • 2-hour Hackathon
  • 5 problems, no negative marking
  • Each problem max 10 marks

Zeta Category (Classes 8-10)

  • 35 SCQ questions (+2/-0.5)
  • 5 MCQ questions (+4/-2)
  • 1 hour test
  • 35 SCQ (+2/-0.5)
  • 5 MCQ (+4/-2)
  • 3 Proofs (0 ≤ x ≤ 10) with 5 alternatives
  • 2 hour test
  • 2.5-hour Hackathon
  • 7 problems, no negative marking
  • Each problem max 10 marks
