Olympiad Formats

Epsilon

  • WBO, WCO, WPO, WSO, WJSO:
    25 SCQ (+2/-0.5), 5 MCQ (+4/-2), 45 min test
  • WMO: 25 SCQ (+2/-0.5), 5 MCQ (+4/-2), 1 Proof (0≤x≤10, 2 alternatives), 1hr
  • WHO: 2hr Hackathon, 5 problems, 10 marks each, no negative marking

Zeta

  • WBO, WCO, WPO, WSO, WJSO:
    35 SCQ (+2/-0.5), 5 MCQ (+4/-2), 1hr test
  • WMO: 35 SCQ (+2/-0.5), 5 MCQ (+4/-2), 3 Proofs (0≤x≤10, 5 alternatives), 2hr
  • WHO: 2.5hr Hackathon, 7 problems, 10 marks each, no negative marking
